Bulls and Cows

You have to guess a 4-digit secret number that is automatically generated.

After you guess, you will be told how many digits from your number are in the correct place (bulls), and how many digits from your number are in the wrong place, but are still in the secret number (cows).

Digits from the secret number can be repeated (1123 would be a valid secret number, for example).

You win if you get 4 bulls within 8 attempts.
Good luck!
